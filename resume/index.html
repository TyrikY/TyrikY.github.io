Contact Info

  • Send a signal: Knuckles values his duty, a signal indicating the Master Emerald is in danger will grab his attention.
  • Earn his respect: Knuckles is all about trust and integrity. Showing him your noble intentions will often lead to his help.
  • Through Sonic and friends: If you find a way to contact Sonic or Tails will help you find Knuckles.

Education

  • Knuckles does not have any form of formal education. I relies on his abilities and experiences to guide him.

Experience

  • Isolated Life on Angel Island: He spent most of his life on Angel Island, surrounded by nature and other animals.
  • Limited Socail Interaction: He has limited contact with the outside world, primarily focusing on his duty.
  • Strong Sense of Duty and Responsible: He is driven by a strong sense of duty and responsibility, which guide his actions and decisions.
  • Fierce Protector: He is fiercely protective of the Master Emerald and will do anything to ensure its safety.

Skills
